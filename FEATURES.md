# Student Marketplace App: Feature List

This document outlines the planned features for the application, categorized by user impact area.

---

### 1. User Authentication & Trust
-   **Verified Student Accounts:** Mandatory login with a `.edu` email address to ensure a trusted community.
-   **Alternate Login:** Option to log in using a verified phone number.
-   **Student ID Verification (Optional):** An optional layer of verification for added trust.
-   **User Profiles:** Shows username, profile picture, department, graduation year, join date, and average rating.
-   **Ratings & Reviews:** 5-star rating system with public comments for both buyers and sellers after a transaction.
-   **Report & Block System:** Easy-to-access buttons on listings and profiles to report inappropriate content or block users.

---

### 2. Item Listings & Transactions
-   **Create Listings:** Easily create listings with:
    -   Multiple photo/video uploads.
    -   A clear title and detailed description.
    -   Pre-defined categories (e.g., `Textbooks`, `Electronics`, `Dorm Furniture`, `Vehicles`, `Notes`).
    -   Condition tags (e.g., `New`, `Like New`, `Used`).
    -   Pricing options: `Fixed Price`, `Negotiable`, or `Swap`.
    -   Suggested campus pickup locations (hostel, department, etc.).
-   **In-App Chat:** Real-time messaging between buyers and sellers with:
    -   Image sharing.
    -   Read/unread indicators.
-   **Mark as Sold:** A simple button for sellers to move a listing from active to sold.

---

### 3. Search & Discovery
-   **Keyword Search:** Search for items by their name or description.
-   **Advanced Filtering:** Filter search results by category, price range, and condition.
-   **Sorting Options:** Sort results by newest, lowest price, or closest location.
-   **Wishlist & Alerts:** Add a desired item to a wishlist and get a notification if a matching item is listed.

---

### 4. Notifications
-   **Chat Notifications:** Receive an alert for new messages.
-   **Item Activity:** Get notified when someone likes or saves your item.
-   **Transaction Updates:** Alerts for when your item is sold.
-   **Price Alerts:** Notifications for price drops on items you have saved.
